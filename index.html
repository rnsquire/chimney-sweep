Located in Lynton, on the beautiful north Devon coast, Ian Rumbelow provides chimney sweeping service to all communities within the Exmoor National Parks and beyond.

Ian is professionally training by the National Association of Chimney Sweeps (NACS) and offers a range of up to data specialist chimney sweep services that include traditional sweeps, high powered rotary sweeps, CCTV inspection, birds nest removal and smoke tests on stoves, open fires, wood burners, ray burns, agas and Inglenooks.

Ian pride himself in providing a friendly, thorough, and reliable service to all his customers and guarantees to leave your home dust and smoke free.

Rumbelow's Chimney Sweep are fully insured and can provide an industry standard insurance certificate that is recognised by house insurance companies.
